# Privacy Policy

**Milo Bot** — Last updated: March 3, 2026

## What Data We Collect

When you use Milo, we collect and store:

- Your Telegram user ID, username, and name
- Health and fitness data you choose to connect from Whoop (recovery scores, HRV, sleep metrics, strain)
- Body composition data you choose to connect from Withings (weight, body fat percentage, muscle mass)
- Workout logs you submit through the bot
- Messages you send to Milo for coaching purposes

## How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

- Provide personalized fitness, nutrition, sleep, and lifestyle coaching
- Track your progress over time
- Generate data-driven training and recovery recommendations

We do not sell, share, or distribute your personal data to any third parties.

## Third-Party Services

Milo integrates with the following services to function:

- **Whoop** — To retrieve your recovery, sleep, and strain data (via OAuth)
- **Withings** — To retrieve your body composition data (via OAuth)
- **Anthropic** — To process your messages and generate coaching responses
- **Supabase** — To securely store your user profile and fitness data

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We only request the minimum data scopes needed to provide coaching.

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored in a secure Supabase (PostgreSQL) database
- API keys and tokens are encrypted and never exposed publicly
- OAuth tokens for Whoop and Withings are stored securely and used only to fetch your data

## Data Deletion

You can request deletion of all your data at any time by contacting the developer. Upon request, all stored data associated with your Telegram account will be permanently removed.
